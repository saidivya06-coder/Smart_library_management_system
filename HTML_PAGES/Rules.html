Rules & Guidelines

  • Silence & cleanliness should be strictly maintained in & around the Library & Information Centre.
  • After obtaining the Institute Identity Card, all students should apply for Library Membership using the prescribed form available at the information desk.
  • All UG / PG students can borrow four (4) books for a period of 14 days.
  • PG students can also borrow one back volume of a journal for 3 days. Defaulters will be charged Rs.10 per day or face withdrawal of the card.
  • All students and staff must clear dues to obtain a No-Due Certificate.
  • Users must check books/journals for damage before borrowing and report any issues to library staff.
  • Students must get borrowed books checked out at the exit gate.
  • Avoid marking or damaging books. Borrowers will be held responsible for any damage.
  • If a book is lost, it must be replaced with the latest edition or paid for as per rules.
  • Books must be returned on or before the due date. Late returns will be fined Rs.1 per day.
  • Book Bank facility is available for SC/ST students.
  • Book Bank books are issued at the beginning of the semester and must be returned at the end.
  • Reference books and current journals are for in-library use only.
  • Check the notice board regularly for updates.
  • Visit the library website for more information on resources and services.
  • Consult library staff for any clarification.
  • Maintain discipline, proper communication, and cooperation at all times.
  • For suggestions or issues, contact the librarian or email: cbitlic@gmail.com / librarian@cbit.ac.in
