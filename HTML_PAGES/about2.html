Smart Library is another miracle of technology as it is known as an automated and advanced library system that involves the latest technological advancements. This strategy is developed to improve the utilisation of libraries and encourage more frequent visits.

A smart library system can function without the involvement of employees, reducing paperwork and improving efficiency. It manages book records, issue/return, and other operations automatically.

You know that managing a vast collection of books, magazines, and journals can be quite challenging for the library staff. It demands significant time and resources too. Comprehensive automation makes tasks like book procurement, categorisation, serial control, binding, and stock verification quick and simple. This highly increases productivity while also saving library employees from the burden of unnecessary physical labour. Also, the technology removes the possibility of human error which is another outstanding benefit. Ultimately this approach results in smoother, more dependable, and ultimately more successful library operations.
